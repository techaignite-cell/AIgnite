Privacy Policy

Privacy Dashboard · Last updated: April 22, 2026

🔒 This app works completely offline. No data is ever sent to any server.

📱 What This App Does

Privacy Dashboard is an Android app that lets you view and analyse the permissions granted to installed apps on your device — such as camera, microphone, and location. Everything happens locally on your phone.

🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

We collect absolutely nothing. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Your list of installed apps
  • Location, camera, or microphone data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking tokens of any kind

💾 Local Storage Only

The only thing stored on your device is your list of starred/favourite apps. This is saved locally using Android's built-in storage. It never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

🔗 Third-Party Services

None. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. No Firebase, no Facebook SDK, no ads.

🌐 Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests. All permission scanning happens entirely on-device.

👧 Children's Privacy

Since we collect no information from anyone, there is no risk to children. The app is safe for all ages.
